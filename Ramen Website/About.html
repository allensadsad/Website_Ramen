ABOUT US

A エージ RAMEN is a restaurant that specializes in serving
ramen, a Japanese dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat
noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often
flavored with soy sauce or miso, and topped with
ingredients such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, and green
onions Their menu selections vary among respective
branches. We use chef-selected flour to make their
homemade noodles and extract umami from pork bone
into the soup through a unique multi-process method.
They are passionate about providing Japan quality ramen
every day
